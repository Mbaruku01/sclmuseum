About Us

SHIMULIMULI COMPANY LIMITED is a cultural, media, and technology enterprise dedicated to preserving, producing, and disseminating knowledge, heritage, and creative expression through modern digital platforms.

Corporate Identity

SHIMULIMULI COMPANY LIMITED is a legally registered company operating within the United Republic of Tanzania. The company functions as the founding and operating body behind the SCL Digital Museum & Archive, ensuring institutional integrity, professional governance, and long-term sustainability.

  • Registered Company: SHIMULIMULI COMPANY LIMITED
  • Registered Office: Mainland Tanzania
  • Operating Under National Laws
  • Local Roots, Global Reach

Sound recording and music publishing activities.

Motion picture projection and exhibition activities.

Conducting cultural workshops within and outside Tanzania.

Writing, publishing, selling, and supplying books and documentaries in audio and video formats.

Our Vision

To become a leading cultural and digital media institution in Africa, setting standards in heritage preservation, creative production, and knowledge dissemination for present and future generations.
