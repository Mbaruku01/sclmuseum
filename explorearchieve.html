Degombora Zakaria Kitomari

Custodian of Meru cultural practices, oral history, and traditional healing knowledge based in Meru District, Arusha Region.

This artifact documents Meru traditional healing knowledge, community memory, and oral history preserved through interviews and visual records.

Degombora Zakaria Kitomari is a highly respected Meru traditional expert specializing in herbal medicine, healing rituals, and spiritual practices.

Her knowledge has been transmitted orally across generations and remains essential to cultural preservation and education.
