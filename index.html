Artifact of the Week

Sculpting History: Dinosaurs from Metal Waste

Origin: TRIASSIC PERIOD, 245 - 230 Million Years Ago | Location: National Museum of Tanzania

Metal wastes were carefully collected and repurposed to create a welded sculpture in the shape of a dinosaur. This approach not only transforms discarded materials into a striking piece of art but also serves as a tangible preservation of history, connecting viewers with the prehistoric past in a modern, sustainable form. Each welded piece reflects both creativity and conservation, highlighting how cultural memory and environmental awareness can come together in artistic expression
